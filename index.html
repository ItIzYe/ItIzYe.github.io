About ItIzYe


ItIzYe is an up-and-coming artist from the South of Germany with a passion for creating music that speaks to his listeners. He has been making music since he was just a teenager, crafting beats in his bedroom studio and releasing them on SoundCloud.


His sound blends elements of hip hop, R&B, pop, and EDM into one unique style all its own. With lyrical content focused on topics like love and heartbreak, personal growth, and social issues, ItIzYe's songs are sure to evoke emotion in any listener. 


With no signs of slowing down anytime soon, ItIzYe is ready to take the world by storm with his unique blend of musical styles.
